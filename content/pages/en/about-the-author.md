## Yorgo Petsas

Multilingual IT and eCommerce professional based in **Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain**. Head of eCommerce Digital Innovation at **EDEL (Electrónica de Elevadores, S.L.)**, with a long track record across online retail, technical delivery, and cross-functional leadership.

## Background

Roughly **15+ years** in eCommerce management (B2B and B2C) and web development, with growing depth in **programming, data analysis, AI, and cloud technologies**. Comfortable bridging product, marketing, and engineering: from operations and SEO to custom stacks, integrations, and delivery with modern tooling.

**Technical practice (high level):** Python, R, C, SQL, JavaScript, React, Git; cloud exposure across Google, Azure, and Oracle; data and analytics tooling (e.g. Tableau, notebooks, common ML ecosystems); Agile / Scrum.

**Broader digital craft:** Analytics, technical SEO, paid and lifecycle marketing, and hands-on work across major commerce platforms (Magento / Adobe Commerce, WordPress, Shopify, BigCommerce, and others).

**Education:** Bachelor’s in Business Administration (New Bulgarian University). Intensive programming training at **[42](https://www.42network.org/)** (project-based, peer learning).

## Why this book

This project combines **years of shipping real systems** with a focused study of how **AI agents** are designed: loops, tools, memory, planning, and production concerns. The goal is practical: help readers understand how agents work and how to build responsibly on their own machines—not theory for its own sake.
