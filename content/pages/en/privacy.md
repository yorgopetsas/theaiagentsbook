## Privacy notice

When you submit an email on our free-book landing pages, we collect limited lead data to deliver the PDF and measure campaign performance.

## What we collect

- Email address
- Audience segment (CEO, agency, SMB, hybrid)
- Landing page path
- UTM parameters (`utm_source`, `utm_medium`, `utm_campaign`, `utm_content`)
- Submission timestamp

## Why we collect it

- Send or grant access to the free PDF
- Understand which campaigns and pages perform best
- Improve future content and offers

## Where data is stored

Lead data is processed by Cloudflare Worker endpoints and stored in Cloudflare D1 under our account.

## Data retention

We keep lead data only as long as needed for campaign operations and reporting.

## Your rights

To request correction or deletion of your data, contact the project owner via your campaign contact channel.
