## How iteration creates intelligence over time

This chapter focuses on the “loop” that turns single-shot model outputs into multi-step behaviors: think, act, observe, reflect.

## Key idea

Agents get better through repeated cycles, guided by constraints and evaluation—not just by larger prompts.
